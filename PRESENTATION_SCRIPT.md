## 💡 **DELIVERY TIPS:**

### **What to Do:**
✅ **Speak confidently but naturally** — this is your work; you love it  
✅ **Make eye contact** with the audience  
✅ **Pause after key points** — let them sink in  
✅ **Use your screens/slides** to back up what you're saying  
✅ **Keep energy high** — enthusiasm is contagious  
✅ **Practice pacing** — don't rush, don't drag  
✅ **Smile** — you're proud of this work, show it!

### **What to Avoid:**
❌ Don't read word-by-word like a robot  
❌ Don't go too deep into technical jargon if your audience isn't technical  
❌ Don't pause awkwardly — keep talking smoothly  
❌ Don't spend too much time on one project  
❌ Don't apologize for things ("Sorry, this is not perfect")

## 📌 **QUICK PRACTICE ROUTINE** (Before presenting):
1. **Read the script aloud** 2-3 times (feel the flow)
2. **Time yourself** — aim for 2:15 to 2:25
3. **Remove/add content** if you're going over or under
4. **Practice in front of a mirror or friend**
5. **Mark your pauses** in the script with `[PAUSE for effect]`
6. **Practice smooth transitions** between sections
