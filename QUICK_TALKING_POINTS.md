## 🚀 **CONFIDENCE POWER MOVES**

1. **Before you start:** Take a deep breath, smile, make eye contact
2. **During presentation:** Speak clearly, pause after key points (don't rush!)
3. **If you forget:** "Let me come back to that..." or "I'll show you in the code"
4. **If someone asks something technical:** It's OK to say "That's a great question; let me think..." 
5. **End strong:** "Thanks for letting me share this. Feel free to reach out if you want to collaborate!"

---

**Remember:** You've built amazing stuff. Your confidence should come from THAT, not from perfect delivery. 💪
