## 💡 **PACING TIPS**

- **Don't scroll too fast** — let people see what's on screen
- **Read the text on screen** briefly, don't spend minutes on one project
- **Emphasize challenges & solutions** — this is what impresses
- **Use "here, look at this" tone** — not "and this thing does..."
- **Pause after showing something cool** — let it sink in

---

## 🎪 **IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG**

| Problem | What to Do |
|---------|-----------|
| Website doesn't load | "Let me pull up my GitHub instead" → Show code |
| Stuttering while speaking | Take a breath, say "Sorry, let me rephrase that" |
| Person asks technical question you don't know | "That's a great question. I haven't explored that yet, but let me look into it" |
| You lose your place | Take a sip of water, scroll back to where you were, continue |
| Time is running out | Skip the least important projects, jump to closing |

---

## ✨ **FINAL PRO TIPS**

1. **Point at screen while talking** — draws attention to what you're saying
2. **Say "check this out"** — creates excitement for next thing
3. **Use varied pace** — don't monotone through everything
4. **Show your face in camera** — not just the screen
5. **End with energy** — don't deflate at the end, finish strong!
